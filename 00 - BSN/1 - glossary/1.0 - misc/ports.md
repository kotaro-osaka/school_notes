# Ports

> [TCP & UDP Ports](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_TCP_and_UDP_port_numbers)
## Scopes
Well-known Ports: `0-1023`
Registered Ports: `1024-49.151`
Dynamic / Private Ports: `49.152-65.535`
