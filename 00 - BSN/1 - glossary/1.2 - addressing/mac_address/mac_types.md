# MAC Address Types
___
## Unicast
- Unique OUI
- Used to identify specific network interface
## Multicast
> IPv4 prefix: `01-00-5E`
> IPv6 prefix: `33-33`
- Used to communicate to group of devices subscribed to multicast group
## Broadcast
> `FF-FF-FF-FF-FF-FF`
- Used to communicate to all devices on local (sub)net
